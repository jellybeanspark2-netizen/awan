# Electricity Bill Calculator & Tracker (Awan Aaram) ⚡

This is my mobile application project for calculating and tracking electricity bills, developed using **Android Studio**, **Dart**, and the **Flutter** framework. 

I designed and built this app to help people easily understand their electricity costs, manage their household appliances, and save their monthly billing records.

---

## 📱 Features I Built

### 1. Bill Estimator (Calculator)
*   **Dual Calculation Methods**: You can calculate bills with a simple flat rate or with the official **Tiered Slab Tariff** system (e.g., 0-100 units at a certain rate, and higher units at other rates).
*   **Real-time Cost Calculation**: Instantly computes base costs, fixed service charges, and local taxes/surcharges as you type your meter readings.
*   **Visual Usage Gauge**: Displays a simple color-coded bar (Green for low usage, Orange for medium, Red for high usage) to show how much power is being consumed.

### 2. Household Appliance Simulator
*   **Appliance Database**: Users can add their home appliances with their wattage, quantity, and daily hours of operation.
*   **Monthly Unit Calculations**: Automatically computes the estimated monthly kWh usage of each appliance.
*   **Usage Share Percentage**: Displays a visual progress bar for each device showing its exact share of total electricity consumption.

### 3. Saved Billing History
*   **Local Storage Database**: Saves estimated bills securely in local memory.
*   **Breakdown Sheet**: Tap on any saved history card to open a modal dialog with the complete breakdown of that month's bill (readings, base cost, taxes, total).
*   **Record Management**: Easily delete old bills.
